Crazy Motorcycle is a fun and enjoyable motorcycle driving game where you can quickly reach your checkpoints with your Nubik character on simple tracks. Jump over gaps and avoid obstacles on these short snack tracks. The controls in this simple game are also very simple, move or jump the biker in four directions. You will want this game, where the cube visual style meets the crazy arcade theme, to never end!
